
# Multiplayer Gaming with TV Mode Integration

TV mode takes the concept of stream mode to the next level by offering advanced integrations. This exclusive service is specifically designed for games deployed directly on Playroom (upcoming). With TV mode integration, you have the ability to initiate stream mode directly from your phone and leverage Airplay functionality to display the host screen on a TV. Through a shared code/link, every player can seamlessly join the game, enhancing the communal experience and providing an immersive gaming environment on the big screen
