## Multiplayer Kit

### Monthly Active Users (MAU)
To keep it simple, Monthly Active Users is how we charge. MAU are unique users who opened or played your game in the past month from a new device. For our Multiplayer Kit, we donâ€™t charge you for anything else; no server uptime costs or hidden fees.

### Billing Examples
- You are a Student building games, your total bill is Zero
- Your game is non-commercial (no in game purchases or ads), your total bill is Zero
- You are an independent game developer or an indie studio with no revenue, you total can be Zero (Contact Us)
- You have a game that has less than 15k monthly active users, you will be on $10/month Starter plan.
- You have a game that has around 100k active users, you will be on $150/month Pro plan.
- You have a game that has more than 100k users, you will be 150/month + 0.02 for any user above 100k.
- You have a game that has 500k+ MAU, contact us and we can set a pricing that works for you.
